To copy files from Windows to WSL:
cp -r /mnt/c/Users.../Directory_to_copy ~/Directory_to_paste

To later update changes back into Windows:
rsync -av --progress ~/Directory_to_paste /mnt/c/Users.../Directory_to_copy

To check something "pv" Pipe Viewer - Small Linux utility to see the progress of data moving through a pipe or being written to a file:
pv -tpreb <file_being_process> > /dev/null
    - t = Show elapsed time
    - p = Show progress bar
    - r = Show data transfer rate
    - e = Show estimate time
    - b = Show total bytes transferred

Or to see real progress:
cd /mnt/c/Users/Sergi/Documents/GitHub/AB
tar -cf - P7 | pv -s $(du -sb P7 | awk '{print $1}') | tar -xf - -C ~/
